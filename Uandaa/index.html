About the App

Uandaa is a voice-based language exchange app developed by Eratzin Software. It is designed for members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, offering a safe and uplifting space to practice languages, share experiences, and build meaningful friendships.

Key Features

  • Group voice chat rooms
  • Real-time language practice
  • Faith-centered community environment
  • In-app reporting and moderation tools

Safety & Moderation

Uandaa is committed to maintaining a respectful and safe community. Users may report inappropriate content directly within the app. Reports are reviewed promptly, and appropriate action is taken, including content removal or account suspension.
